How it Works?

This simple app computes the day when you can start profit-taking from your TITANO holdings, given some inputs like the initial wallet amount, desired monthly amount you want to take, slippage fees and taxes.
The idea behind this is to calculate from which point in time you can start having rebases that, on average, will sum up to the desired profit taking amount (plus fees & taxes) oveer a month. But since it's an average, and the real autocompound formula does actually increase (*), every month you'll end up with "a little more" than what you would need to withdraw your desired sum. Thus, the balance in your wallet will still grow!
(*)assuming TITANO price will NOT drop
