How it Works?

Given some inputs like your initial TITANOs holdings in your wallet, desired monthly take-profit amount, slippage fees and taxes, this page will compute the date from which you'll be able to withdraw your preconfigured take-profit amount AND have enough "critical mass" of TITANOs left to be self-sustainable, every month.

⚠️NOTE⚠️ This calculation works under the assumption that TITANO price will be the pretty much constant; in future releases I might as well add an extra parameter to take that into account.

(*)assuming TITANO price will NOT drop
